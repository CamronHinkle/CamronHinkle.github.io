As a lifelong artist, and a lover of all things creative my design career began in high school with the National Art Honors Society. I quickly climbed the ranks in this student-run organization ultimately becoming president. From there I was exposed to the true underlying power of visual communication. My drive to better the community fed my curiosity for understanding human interaction and the fundamental elements that form an experience. Once I discovered that these elements of design could be controlled I was hooked. From there I transitioned from helping peers with creative decisions to teaching Art after school. However, I didn't stop there but rather continued learning and moved to Chicago to pursue my passion.
I have experience with traditional and digital design, working to create interactive prototypes, conducting user research, completing web designs, and implementing my designs into final products.
