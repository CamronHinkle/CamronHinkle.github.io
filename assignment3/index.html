Directions

Begin your journey by making your way to Ehime Prefecture. Whether you're coming by train, bus, or foot, navigate toward the heart of Matsuyama.
Locate the abandoned Shinto shrine: Once in the old train station, find Miyazaki Street. At night it becomes a bustling thoroughfare filled with enchanting shops and magical landmarks for all of the spirits.
Follow the Spirited Aroma: Yubaba's Bathhouse is known for its distinctive aroma, a blend of magical herbs and soothing fragrances. Follow this captivating scent as it wafts through Miyazaki Street, guiding you to your destination.
Look for the soribashi or moon bridge: Yubaba's Bathhouse is adorned with unique and mystical symbols. Keep an eye out for these symbols on the buildings along Miyazaki Street. They serve as beacons, leading you closer to the magical haven.

Our mission is to provide a sanctuary for both spirits and humans alike, fostering harmony and healing through the soothing waters and magical atmosphere. We are dedicated to preserving the timeless traditions of Japanese bathhouse culture, where every visitor is welcomed with respect, empathy, and a transformative experience that transcends the boundaries between worlds.

Manager

Yubaba is the formidable proprietor of the bathhouse in Spirited Away. Known for her commanding presence and powerful magic, she oversees the establishment with strict rules. Despite her stern exterior, Yubaba possesses complex layers, balancing her business acumen with a softer side, particularly towards her baby son, Boh. While Yubaba is the primary owner, her twin sister, Zeniba, also plays a role in the bathhouse's dynamics, showcasing a more benevolent and understanding nature. The proprietors, with their unique personalities, contribute to the bathhouse's intricate and magical world.
